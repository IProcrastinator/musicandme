The Story

Music and Me is a powerful Filipino duo of two exceptional event and concert-based singers from Bohol: Fedrianne Quilantang Villanueva and Jennifer Ann "J-Ann" Talisic that captured the hearts of the nation as a Grand Finalist on the first season of The Voice Generations Philippines in 2023. Mentored by Coach Julie Anne San Jose, they were a part of the esteemed Julesquad.

Us Band

While Fedrianne and J-Ann are the core singers recognized as "Music and Me" from the competition, they often perform alongside a talented instrumental group known as "Us," which is why they are frequently called "Music and Me and Us."

  • Guitarist: Janwel Ladaga
  • Bassist: Ermar Vince Ladaga
  • Drummer: Rigo Onque Viñalon
  • Keyboardist: Gino Ejara Victorio
  • Percussionist (New Member): Karl Erik Peligrino

Together, Fedrianne and J-Ann (Music and Me) and their instrumentalists (Us) are a dynamic collective showcasing the raw and polished talent of Bohol and the Visayan region. Their journey on the show was marked by memorable performances, including a four-chair-turn Blind Audition with their rendition of "Makita Kang Muli" and a captivating, theater-like performance of "Defying Gravity" that earned them a spot in the finals.
